# aggressive

adj. 侵略性的,激进的
Ready or likely to attack or confront; behaving in a forceful or competitive way.

# 例句

The patient has an **aggressive** form of cancer that requires immediate treatment.

病人患有一种侵袭性癌症，需要立即治疗。
