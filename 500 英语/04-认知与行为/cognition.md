# cognition

n. 认知
The mental action or process of acquiring knowledge and understanding through thought, experience, and the senses.

# 例句

Sleep plays a vital role in **cognition** and memory consolidation.

睡眠在认知和记忆巩固中起着至关重要的作用。

# 派生
#### cognitive 
adj. 认知的
#### cognizant 
adj. 认知的,知晓的
