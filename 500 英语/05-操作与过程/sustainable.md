# sustainable

adj. 可持续的
Able to be maintained at a certain rate or level; avoiding depletion of natural resources.

# 例句

**Sustainable** development meets present needs without compromising future generations.

可持续发展在满足当前需求的同时不损害后代。

# 派生
#### sustain 
v. 维持
#### sustainability
