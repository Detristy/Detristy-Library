# hypothesis

n. 假说
A supposition or proposed explanation made on the basis of limited evidence as a starting point.

# 例句

The scientist formulated a **hypothesis** to explain the unusual phenomenon.

科学家提出了一个假说来解释这个不寻常的现象。
